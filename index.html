The Process

Efficient, Effortless,
Professional

Designed around the reality of busy organizations. Minimal disruption, maximum results. Your team is back to work quickly — with images that matter.

1

Schedule the Session

We coordinate directly with your team or event organizers. Logistics handled from day one — timing, space requirements, and session flow.

2

Professional On-Site Setup

Jeff arrives with complete lighting and backdrop equipment. Setup is compact and unobtrusive — your office keeps running without missing a beat.

3

Guided Posing & Coaching

Every subject receives expert direction on posture, expression, and presence. Jeff's coaching brings out a confident, natural look in everyone — no matter how camera-shy.

4

Fast Delivery of Final Images

Polished, retouched images delivered via private online gallery. Production-ready for websites, directories, and press materials.

About Jeff

I'm Here
to Help

There is no better feeling than helping people succeed. A great headshot is more than a photograph — it's a tool that can open doors, earn opportunities, and project confidence long before you say a word. Helping someone walk away with an image that genuinely represents who they are at their best is what drives me.

I've been in the professional photography industry for 12 years shooting headshots, weddings and portraits. But technical mastery is only a small part of getting an amazing headshot — bringing out the best pose and expression in someone is the magic formula that creates a stunning image. With my coaching, we work together to achieve your absolute best look. It is simple, easy and fun.

Knowing that a photo I created is out there working for someone every single day — every time they hand someone a business card, pull up their LinkedIn profile, or walk into a room where people already know their face — that kind of impact never gets old.

Jeff Sellenrick Headshots offers professional on-location headshots for any team, corporate office, university or business. Also offering individual headshots for LinkedIn, actors, musicians and job seekers. Headshot booths available for trade shows and conferences, with options to deliver results instantly.
