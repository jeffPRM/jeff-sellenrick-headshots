# Jeff Sellenrick Headshots — Website Brief
*Original design prompt, March 2026*

## Core Goal
Attract corporate clients and organizations needing high-quality headshots at scale (law firms, corporate offices, real estate brokerages, conferences, trade shows, professional associations), while clearly offering an option for individual professionals (LinkedIn, personal branding, speaking engagements).

## Brand Feel
Premium, professional, trustworthy. Similar to top consulting firms, law firm websites, or luxury creative studios. Serious but welcoming. Not playful, trendy, gimmicky, or wedding-photography oriented.

## Visual Design
- Minimal, structured, editorial
- Alternating dark/light section blocks for scroll rhythm
- Strong typography carries much of the design weight

## Color Palette (Original Brief)
- Soft off-white — light sections
- Deep charcoal / near-black — dark sections
- Neutral grays — supporting elements
- **Orange** — primary CTA accent (used sparingly: buttons, important links, key highlights)

*Note: During build, orange was replaced with Teal to match Jeff's actual brand. See current index.html for final color decisions.*

## Typography
- Headings: refined serif or sophisticated modern sans serif
- Body: clean, highly readable sans serif
- Final pairing used: Cormorant Garamond + DM Sans

## Page Sections (in order)
1. **Hero** — Large headshot image, headline ("Corporate Headshots That Elevate Your Brand"), subheadline, primary CTA (Book Headshots), secondary CTA (View Portfolio)
2. **Trusted By Professionals** — Logos/references to industries and companies served
3. **Services** — 4 cards: Corporate Headshots, Conference & Event, Office & Team Photography, Individual Headshots
4. **Portfolio** — Grid of high-end headshots, hover interactions, mix of corporate/individual
5. **How It Works** — 4 steps: Schedule → Professional Setup → Guided Posing → Fast Delivery
6. **Individual Headshots** — Dedicated section for solo bookings (LinkedIn, personal branding, career)
7. **About** — Jeff's bio, experience, workflow efficiency, portrait
8. **Testimonials** — Quotes from real clients (Lauren, Justin, Fabien, Allison, Jonathan)
9. **Final CTA** — Dark background, "Professional Headshots for Your Team or Your Career", Book + Quote buttons
10. **Footer**

## Key Design Principles
- Clean layout, generous spacing
- Strong typography
- Minimal color palette
- Elegant image presentation
- Subtle motion and hover interactions
- Appropriate for law firms, corporate leadership, conferences, real estate brokerages
