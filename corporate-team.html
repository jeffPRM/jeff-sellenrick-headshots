Every Team Session Includes

Everything Your Team
Needs

Full On-Site Setup

Professional lighting, backdrop, and equipment set up at your office or location. No travel burden on your team — Jeff brings everything to you.

Expression & Posing Coaching

Every person receives individual direction — posture, expression, presence. Jeff's coaching makes the experience feel relaxed and natural, even for people who dislike being photographed.

On-Site Image Selection

Each team member reviews and selects their final image during the session. They leave knowing exactly what they'll receive — no post-session debates or re-shoot requests.

Professional Retouching

Every selected image is professionally retouched — clean, natural, and production-ready. Enhancing the best version of each person without heavy-handed editing.

Individual Secure Delivery

Each team member receives their own retouched image via a personal, secure gallery link. No bulk downloads for you to sort through and redistribute — it's handled.

Consistent Style Across All Images

Uniform lighting, backdrop, crop, and retouching style across every person in the group. Whether it's 7 people or 70, the images will look like they belong together.

The Process

Efficient, Effortless,
Professional

01

Schedule the Session

We coordinate directly with you around your team's schedule. Timing, space requirements, and session flow are handled from day one — you send a date and a headcount.

02

Professional Setup

Jeff arrives with complete lighting and backdrop equipment. All that's needed is a conference room or open space — the rest of the office keeps running without disruption.

03

Coached Individual Sessions

Each team member spends 5–10 minutes in front of the camera. Jeff guides posture, expression, and presence throughout. Each person selects their favorite image before leaving.

04

Retouched & Delivered

Polished, retouched images are delivered via individual secure gallery links. Each person receives their own image directly — no distribution burden on you.

For the Person Running This

We Handle the Details

Coordinating headshots for a team takes real effort. This process is built to handle as much of that as possible.

Scheduling Support

We work with you to build a session flow around your team's calendar. Whether rotating people through in small groups or reserving windows for leadership, the timing is built around your day — not ours.

Ready-to-Send Team Communication

We provide messaging you can send directly to your team — what to wear, what to expect, how long it takes, how they'll receive their images. No repeated questions for you to field.

Individual Delivery — Handled

Single Point of Contact

From booking to final delivery, you work directly with Jeff. No assistants to track down, no third-party platforms to navigate. One conversation, start to finish.
